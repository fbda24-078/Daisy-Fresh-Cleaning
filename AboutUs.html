Our Story

Daisy Fresh Cleaning began with one man, a mop, and a dream. In 2011, our founder, Phenyo, noticed a gap in the local market for dependable, detail-oriented cleaning services. With a passion for cleanliness and a strong work ethic, he decided to start small — offering cleaning services to neighbors, friends, and small offices in Francistown.

What started as a side hustle quickly grew through word-of-mouth. Phenyo’s commitment to excellence, punctuality, and trustworthy service made Daisy Fresh Cleaning a household name. From scrubbing tiles to revitalizing carpets, his hands-on approach and friendly demeanor left a lasting impression on every client.

Over the years, Daisy Fresh evolved into a fully-fledged cleaning company serving both residential and commercial clients across Botswana. Despite the growth, we’ve never lost our personal touch. Every cleaner on our team shares Phenyo’s original values — integrity, consistency, and care in every job.

Today, Daisy Fresh Cleaning is proud to be a family-oriented business. We treat every space we clean as if it were our own, and every customer like family. Whether it’s a weekly home clean, a post-renovation scrub-down, or a deep office refresh, we go above and beyond to make your environment shine.

Our Values

We believe that true success is built on trust and community. That’s why we live by these core values:

  • Quality: We take pride in doing the job right the first time.
  • Reliability: You can count on us to show up, deliver, and exceed expectations.
  • Respect: We treat your space and privacy with the utmost respect.
  • Community: We support local causes and believe in giving back.
